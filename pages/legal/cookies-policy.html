1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to:

  • Make websites work more efficiently
  • Provide information to website owners
  • Enhance your user experience

Cookies do not typically contain personally identifiable information, but personal data we store about you may be linked to information stored in cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

Type: Session Cookies

Administered by: Pixelwave

Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality like user authentication and security features. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Functionality Cookies

Type: Persistent Cookies

Administered by: Pixelwave

Purpose: These cookies remember your preferences (like language or region) to provide enhanced, more personalized features.

Performance Cookies

Type: Analytics Cookies

Administered by: Third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics)

Purpose: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
